_Effective date: July 18, 2026_

IARSDR is an SDR (software-defined radio) receiver application for Android,
developed by Isak (PU3IAR) and offered through [id.qsl.br](https://id.qsl.br).

## Data collection

**IARSDR does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** The
app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. No
account is required and no personal information is requested.

## Permissions and how they are used

- **USB access** — used exclusively to communicate with an RTL-SDR (RTL2832U)
  receiver connected to your device. No data from the dongle leaves your device.
- **Internet** — used only when you choose to connect to an `rtl_tcp` server
  whose address you provide. The app initiates no other network connections.

## Local data

Settings (frequency, gain, language, layout preferences) and any audio/IQ
recordings you create are stored **only on your device**, under the app's own
storage area, and can be deleted by you at any time (in-app or by uninstalling
the app).

## Radio spectrum

The app receives publicly available radio signals through hardware you control.
It does not transmit and does not log tuning activity anywhere outside your
device.
