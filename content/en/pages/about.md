I am Isak, an infrastructure analyst based in Brazil. My day-to-day work is
platform reliability: Kubernetes, infrastructure as code with Terraform, CI/CD on
Google Cloud, and observability with Prometheus, Grafana, and Sentry.

I came to infrastructure from development, after several years writing software —
much of it in Python — before moving to the operations and reliability side.

Outside of work I maintain open-source projects in three areas:

- **Cryptography** — elliptic-curve libraries across Python, JavaScript, and Go
  ([ecutils](https://github.com/isakruas/ecutils)), and an end-to-end encrypted
  chat built on them ([e2hat](https://e2hat.com)).
- **Radio and signal processing** — HF digital modes, modems, and SDR tooling.
  I am a licensed radio amateur, [PU3IAR](https://id.qsl.br/op/PU3IAR).
- **Embedded systems** — a toolchain for 8-bit AVR microcontrollers: a language,
  compiler, emulators, and an IDE.

This site is where I write about that work. The source for everything I publish
is on [GitHub](https://github.com/isakruas).
